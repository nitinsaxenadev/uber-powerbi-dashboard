# 💡 Key Insights — Uber Analytics Dashboard

This document summarises the business insights that can be derived from the dashboard.

---

## 📊 Overview Insights

- **Booking Trends**: Monthly area and column charts reveal peak and off-peak booking periods, helping identify seasonality in ride demand.
- **Booking Status**: Three donut charts show the split between completed, cancelled, and failed bookings — a core metric for operational health.
- **Ratings**: Separate KPI cards for customer and driver ratings provide a quick read on two-sided marketplace satisfaction.

---

## 🚙 Vehicle Insights

- **Top Performers**: The vehicle table ranks vehicle types by Booking Value, Completed Bookings, Customer Count, and Contribution % — making it easy to spot the highest-revenue categories.
- **Vehicle Mix**: Donut charts reveal how booking outcomes differ across vehicle types (e.g., premium vehicles may have lower cancellation rates).

---

## 💰 Revenue Insights

- **Revenue Seasonality**: The monthly area chart shows whether revenue is growing, flat, or declining over time.
- **Payment Mix**: The column chart breaks down revenue by payment method, highlighting whether digital payments (UPI/Card/Wallet) dominate over cash.
- **Top Customers**: The bar chart of top customers by revenue helps identify high-value riders for loyalty or retention programs.

---

## 👤 Rider Insights

- **Rider Segmentation**: Three donut charts split customers into First-Time, Returning, and Regular riders — a key cohort analysis for growth vs. retention strategy.
- **Cancellation Reasons**: The driver cancellation table surfaces the most common reasons for ride failures, pointing to operational improvements.
- **Customer-Level Detail**: The per-customer table enables drill-down into individual rider behaviour — bookings, losses, average trip length.

---

## 📍 Location Insights

- **Demand Hotspots**: The pickup location bar chart identifies the highest-demand areas, useful for driver supply planning.
- **Time-Slot Heatmap**: The Weekday × Time Slot pivot table is the most powerful analytical tool in the report — it reveals exactly when and which days bookings peak, enabling dynamic pricing or driver incentive strategies.
- **Distance by Vehicle**: Shows which vehicle types cover the most distance, useful for fleet planning.

---

## ✅ Recommendations Based on Dashboard

1. **Reduce lost bookings** — Investigate the top driver cancellation reasons and address root causes (e.g., long pickup distances, pricing disputes).
2. **Incentivise digital payments** — If cash dominates the payment mix, nudging riders toward UPI/card reduces friction and improves safety.
3. **Retain regular riders** — The rider segmentation shows repeat behaviour; loyalty perks for regular riders can increase LTV.
4. **Optimise supply for peak slots** — Use the Weekday × Time Slot heatmap to ensure driver availability aligns with demand peaks.
5. **Focus marketing on top pickup zones** — Concentrate acquisition spend near high-demand pickup locations.
